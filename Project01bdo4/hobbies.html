Music

One of my most favorite hobbies is Music. I started playing Music in 4th grade starting with Violin. I later started playing Piano around 11th grade. Since the Piano was too big, I played a smaller Piano, that you blow into, called the Melodica. I met my closest friends thanks to Music and I'm glad I got to meet them and still be able to talk to them today.

Orchestra was one of my favorite electives/classes back in High School. I got to go on many fun trips with them. We got to go to Atlanta, Georgia and see the Aquarium and Coke Factory over there. Next we went to Chicago, Illinois to perform there and see antique string instruments they had and see the Cloud Gate. Lastly, we were able to go to Disney World in Orlando, Florida to compete against other Orchestra as well as have fun at Epcot, Magic Kingdom, and Animal Kingdom. Overall, I had a fun time with my Orchestra and am so thankful that I get to be able to talk to my close friends from there to this day now. I sometimes still play my instruments here and there.

Anime

Animes are my favorite shows to watch whenever I am bored, have free time, or want to watch something. They have all kinds of genres like Fantasy World, Romantic, School Life, Horror, Action, Adventure, etc. These animes are just a few examples of the ones that I have watched, and the ones that I have really enjoyed. But if I were to name every single one, you find that on my anime list. Anime is animations. Specifically from Japan. Anime has recently become very popular in today's world. It's becoming so popular that popular Anime films that would do well in Japan are starting to make their way into America's theatres too.

The first Anime I saw in theatres was Your Name. Your Name has a beautiful story, amazing soundtrack, and beautiful animations. Your Name is a romance and adventure animated film that has the ability to switch bodies with a boy and a girl. The boy and the girl would end up playing each other's lifes and ends up falling for each other. The boy ends up not being able to switch bodies with her anymore, and goes on a journey to find her. What he finds is something you should watch for yourself.

An Anime that got me into watching more Anime was Sword Art Online. Sword Art Online is an anime that focuses on Fantasy World, Action, Adventure, and a bit of Romance. The main protagonist, Kirito, gets stuck inside a video game along with 7,000 other players and the creator made it that if you died in the game, the game would kill your brain, meaning you would die in real time too. The only way to escape the game was to clear all 100 floors and defeating the final boss that the creator created.

Last Anime that I would like to talk about is Fate/Stay Night. Fate/Stay Night is a tricky Anime because it has so many different alternate universes to it. Which makes it really great and more content to watch and know. Fate/Stay Night is an anime with a both of Action. The Anime is about seven masters who get to summon a servant of a great hero in the past to partake in the game to win the Holy Grail. The great hero can be King Arthur, Gilgamesh, Alexander the Great, Hercules, and many more. The masters fight each other to the death and the last master and servant combo gets to win the Holy Grail that grants them one wish.
